Research Directions

Our research group focuses on core problems in Foundation Models, Machine Learning, and Biomedical AI (Theory + Algorithms + Applications), including both novel algorithm and model development, as well as multimodal medical application deployment.

Machine Learning Algorithms

Model and algorithm innovation

  • Video-based Multimodal Foundation Models
  • Fine-grained Temporal Understanding
  • Multimodal Reasoning & Post-training

Multi-modal Foundation Models

Medical multimodal models integrating CT/MRI/Ultrasound/Pathology imaging, clinical structured data, and molecular & genetic data for imaging reasoning and cross-modal diagnostic analysis.

Generative Models

Medical generative models for image synthesis and enhancement, improving reconstruction quality and robustness for downstream diagnostic tasks.

Multimodal Agents

Medical multimodal intelligent agents based on video/imaging/clinical text inputs, building interactive agent frameworks for automated diagnosis, report generation, and clinical decision support, exploring the complete pipeline from models to clinical workflows.
