About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in Biomedical Engineering (BME) at the University of Michigan, College of Engineering. I lead the PixAIL (Precision Imaging AI Laboratory), where we pioneer cutting-edge research in computer vision and biomedical AI. Prior to joining UMich, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Stanford AIMI (Artificial Intelligence in Medicine & Imaging) and earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Northwestern University.

My research at PixAIL lies at the intersection of computer vision and biomedical data analysis, focusing on machine learning challenges arising from healthcare and major diseases. I am dedicated to advancing multimodal biomedical AI technologies, with continuous contributions to medical imaging security, precision disease diagnosis, and personalized medicine, covering critical areas such as Oncology and Cardiology.

PixAIL Research

Digital Cardiology

Developing AI systems to democratize cardiac MRI interpretation by automating cardiac function measurements and providing evidence-based recommendations for cardiovascular disease management. Our goal is to create open-source platforms that enhance CVD diagnosis, particularly in resource-limited settings.

Cardiac MRI CVD Diagnosis Open-Source AI

AI-Enabled Precision Oncology

Extending AI-powered, low-cost presurgical molecular diagnostics to brain tumors and pan-cancer applications. We integrate large language models for clinical report analysis with vision models for medical imaging to enable presurgical risk stratification and revolutionize cancer treatment management.

Precision Oncology Molecular Diagnostics LLM Integration

Healthcare-Driven AI Algorithms

Developing parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods for large models in healthcare settings with limited data. We focus on multi-modality inference, foundational AI models, and federated learning approaches to overcome resource constraints while ensuring model effectiveness and generalizability across diverse healthcare scenarios.

Parameter Efficiency Foundation Models Multi-Modal AI
